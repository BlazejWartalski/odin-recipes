Jurassic Bowl

Dinosaurs and meat-free nuggets? Two of fiancees most loved things combined!

  • Vegan Dinosaur Nuggets
  • Mashed Potatoes
  • Broccoli
  • Peas
  • Gravy
  1. Prepare the mashed potatoes
  2. Shove vegan dinosaurs in the oven for 20 minutes, at 180 degrees
  3. Bake broccoli and peas in the oven for 10 minutes
  4. Get gravy somewhere
  5. Build the Jurassic Island

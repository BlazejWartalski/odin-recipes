Hot Chocolate

Fiancee's favourite drink. Warms up smol bois heart.

  • Milk
  • Hot chocolate powder
  • Marshmallows
  • Chocolate tubes
  • Love
  1. Heat up the milk until its almost boiling. Do not reach the boiling point!
  2. Pour the milk into a mug
  3. Add 2-3 spoons of hot chocolate powder and mix
  4. Decorate with marshmallows on top
  5. Insert some chocolate tubes and love into the drink
